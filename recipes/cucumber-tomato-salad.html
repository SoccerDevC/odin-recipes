Cucumber Tomato Salad

Finished Cucumber tomato salad

With its fine touch its a very nice meal and works great as a salad most especially for family meals

Ingredients

  • oil
  • Salt
  • Sugar
  • Vinegar
  • Water

Steps

  1. Gather all the ingredients
  2. Whisk water, oil, sugar, salt and vinegar in a bowl until the mixture is smooth
  3. Add cucumbers, tomatoes, and onion and stir to coat.
  4. Cover bowl with plastic wrap; refrigerate for at least 2 hours.
